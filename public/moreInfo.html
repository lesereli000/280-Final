Library Services

by Christian Record


Christian Record provides Library Services for legally blind individuals, children and veterans.
This program provides:
- Access to books and magazines and request other resources in a number of formats: refreshable or printed braille, large print text for eReader or paper, and audio files for use on Daisy or streaming over a computer or device. - Audio players to qualified individuals, veterans and children.
